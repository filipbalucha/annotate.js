annotate.js
Annotations, made effortless.

Allow users to highlight and annotate text on your webpage. Annotations persist across sessions and can be easily navigated using the annotate.js navigator component. Annotate.js is lightweight and has no dependencies â€“ not even jQuery!

API & Documentation

Try it out:

  1. Select some text on this page
  2. A tooltip will appear
  3. Pick a predefined highlight color or click on the last one to use your own
  4. Optional: Add a comment
  5. Click on a highlight to modify it
  6. Repeat!
  7. Refresh the page to see that the annotations you just made persist!
  8. Navigate your annotations:
    1. Click on the toggle in the bottom right corner
    2. Optional: Click on a highlight color to filter annotations (click again to reset)
    3. Click on any annotation to be directed to it
